# Privacy Policy for BlueBloc

This Privacy Policy explains how BlueBloc handles information when you use the app.

## 1. Scope

This policy applies to the BlueBloc mobile application.

## 2. Information Stored by the App

BlueBloc stores limited app and gameplay data locally on your device so the app can function properly and preserve your preferences. This may include:

- Best score and best level
- Input mode preference
- Audio and haptics preferences
- Visual and gameplay settings
- Ad cadence preference
- Other similar local settings required for gameplay continuity

This local data is used to support app functionality and improve the player experience.

## 3. Advertising

BlueBloc uses Google AdMob to display ads, including interstitial ads and rewarded ads.

Google and its advertising partners may collect or receive certain information from your device or ad interactions to:

- Deliver ads
- Limit fraud and abuse
- Measure ad performance
- Support rewarded and interstitial ad behavior
- Improve ad relevance where permitted

Examples of data that may be processed by advertising providers can include:

- Device identifiers
- IP address
- Approximate location derived from network information
- Diagnostics and performance data
- Ad interaction events

BlueBloc does not control how Google or its partners process data once it is collected through their SDKs. Their processing is governed by their own policies and terms.

For more information, refer to:

- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Google AdMob / Mobile Ads privacy information: https://developers.google.com/admob/ios/privacy/strategies

## 4. App Tracking Transparency

On supported Apple platforms and OS versions, BlueBloc may present Apple’s App Tracking Transparency prompt before loading ads.

If you allow tracking, the advertising SDK may use the advertising identifier as permitted by Apple and applicable law.

If you do not allow tracking, ads may still be shown, but advertising providers may have reduced access to tracking-related identifiers.

## 5. Analytics and Direct Account Collection

BlueBloc does not require you to create an account.

BlueBloc does not knowingly collect personal information directly from you through a registration form, profile system, or in-app messaging feature.

## 6. Children’s Privacy

BlueBloc is not intended to knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

If you believe information has been provided in a way that raises a privacy concern, contact support and we will review the issue.

## 7. Data Retention

Locally stored gameplay and settings data remains on your device until it is changed, the app is removed, or device/app data is cleared.

Data collected by third-party advertising providers is subject to those providers’ own retention practices and policies.

## 8. Your Choices

You may be able to limit or affect certain data practices by:

- Responding to the App Tracking Transparency prompt
- Adjusting device privacy settings
- Resetting or limiting advertising permissions in iOS or iPadOS
- Removing the app and its local data from your device

## 9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updated versions will be published with the support materials for BlueBloc.
